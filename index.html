Getting Started

Simply type your question in the input field and press send. Harmony AI will respond with the most relevant information from its knowledge base.

Features

  • 20,000+ Knowledge Base: Answers on many topics
  • Chat History: View and manage past conversations
  • Dark Mode: Easier on your eyes at night
  • Export Chats: Save your conversations

Tips

  • Be specific with your questions for better answers
  • Try different phrasings if you don't get the answer you need
  • Use the chat history to revisit important information
